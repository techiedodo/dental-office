We cater to you needs

Chatsworth Dental Group is a full service dental practice that caters to the needs of our patients. Established in 1967, Chatsworth Dental Group has committed to serving the community with exceptional dental care. Using the most innovative technology and procedures, we ensure the highest quality of work for all of our patients. With the expertise of Dr. Vikram Mishra DDS, our team is able to fuse cosmetic dentistry and oral surgery to produce total rehabilitation of oral care.

Services

Prevention: Cleaning, Fluoride Treatments, etc.
Restoration: Fillings, Crowns, Veneers, etc.
Cosmetic: Whitening, Cosmetic Fillings, etc.
Endodontics: Root Canal Therapy, etc.
