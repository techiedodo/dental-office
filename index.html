We cater to your needs

Chatsworth Dental Group is a full service dental practice that caters to the needs of our patients. Established in 1967, Chatsworth Dental Group has committed to serving the community with exceptional dental care. Using the most innovative technology and procedures, we ensure the highest quality of work for all of our patients. With the expertise of Dr. Vikram Mishra DDS, our team is able to fuse cosmetic dentistry and oral surgery to produce total rehabilitation of oral care.

Services

A standard of excellence in personalized dental care enables us to provide the quality dental services our patients deserve. We provide comprehensive treatment planning and use restorative and cosmetic dentistry to achieve your optimal dental health. We are a dental practice devoted to restoring and enhancing the natural beauty of your smile using conservative, state-of-the-art procedures that will result in beautiful, long lasting smiles!

Implants: Provide a strong foundation for fixed or removable replacement teeth that are made to match your natural teeth.

Sleep Apnea Treatment: Minimally invasive devices designed to treat sleep and breathing disorders such as snoring and sleep apnea.

Cosmetic Dentistry: Dentistry aimed at creating a positive change to your teeth and to your smile.

Dental CT: Used to produce three dimensional (3-D) images of teeth, soft tissues, nerve pathways and bone in a single scan.

Laser Dentristry: Used for surgery, some to cure restorative materials and enhance tooth bleaching, and others to remove tooth structure.

Oral Surgery: Focus in treating many diseases, injuries and defects in hard and soft tissues of the mouth.

Meet Our Staff

What makes us different?

As a full service dental practice, Chatsworth Dental Group remains an industry leader. Our mission is to help our patients live healthier lives. We provide a nurturing environment where your health and comfort is of primary importance. Our highly trained staff develops a custom plan catered to your dental needs.

Dr. Vikram Mishra DDS

Dr.Vikram Mishra graduated dentistry in 1995. He received his special training in cosmetic dentistry from UCLA in 2005. He completed his dental implant training in 2006 and received his fellowship award from international congress of oral implantology. His philosophy is to give smile back to people what they deserve. Dr. Mishra fuses his experience in cosmetic dentistry with his skill in dental implant surgery to produce total rehabilitation of oral care. He is becoming popular among patients and community for personable personality and gentle touch in dentistry.
